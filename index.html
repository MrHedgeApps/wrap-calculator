📖 App Walkthrough

🗺️ 1. Finding Your Water

Get your map centered over your swim using one of three options:

  • Search Bar: Type a lake name, town, or postcode. Tap a suggestion to fly there.
  • 📍 My Location: Snaps the map layout straight onto your active bankside position.
  • 🏆 Premier Venues Tab: Select from a pre-loaded matrix of the Top 20 UK fisheries.

🔓 2. Setting Your Pins

By default, the map is locked to prevent misclicks while scrolling.

  • Tap 🔓 Unlock Map to enable editing capabilities.
  • Tap 📍 Peg (turns Orange), then tap your exact swim position on the map satellite imagery.
  • Tap 🎯 Target (turns Red), then tap the spot or feature you are fishing to.
  • Tap ✕ Reset Pins at any time to flush your active lines clean.

📏 3. Selecting Tackle & Results

  • Select your active Rod Length using the dropdown menu below the map.
  • The Target Geometry panel instantly displays the exact distance alongside the exact number of wrap revolutions to clip up to on your distance sticks.
